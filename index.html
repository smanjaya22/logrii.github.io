About Us

LOGRI (local agribusiness) is a B to B business platform that provides a variety of local agricultural products. We are committed to helping farmers by being partners who buy their produce.

LOGRI is a brand from CV. Bale Lombok Nusantara which was established in 2021 in Central Lombok, Indonesia. Our company produces various processed Moringa leaves whose quality and quality is guaranteed. Our products have been certified and passed the quality test so they are safe for consumption.

Vision: ensure the best quality raw materials, Build partners with as many Moringa farmer groups as possible, Guarantee the production process with international standards.

Mission: create quality products that can meet the needs of the world
